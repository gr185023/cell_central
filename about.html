About Us

Smartphones have revolutionized the world of communications as we know it. With hundreds of millions of active smartphones in the world, technology has become constant in many of our lives – we use it to stay connected with our family and friends, to take photos and instantly upload them to our social media, to use as our GPS when we’re travelling, to calculate tips when dining out, to check and book hotels and show times, search for “must-go” places during our trip, etc., etc.

We take our smartphones with us everywhere we go. It’s now becoming more of a necessity, not just luxury, for everyone.

Filipinos are not any different. As a matter of fact, the Philippines is now the fastest-growing market for smartphones in Southeast Asia, with a staggering 326% increase in smartphone sales in 2012. According to the survey, the top functions Filipinos use their smartphones are as follows:

  1. SMS/Text
  2. Taking photos/videos
  3. Social Media
  4. Search Functions
  5. Listening to Music / Playing games

Since cell phones are now so much part of the Fiilipinos’ everyday lives, we find it hard to part with them when we hop on a plane to other places for a vacation, especially other countries like the U.S. But stories about P5,000+ cellphone bills when they return to the Philippines after a quick US visit have scared many consumers into using their smartphones abroad.

There is nothing quite like travelling in the United States. It’s a massive, incredibly diverse and sprawling country. Just an example, California is home to most Filipino-Americans with a population of approximately 1.2 million per the 2010 U.S. Census. With that Fil-Am population, over 10,000 tourists (friends and relatives) from the Philippines come here every year to visit. However, for a country that leads the world in technology, though, the prepaid SIM card market for international visitors has long been surprisingly confusing, limited and expensive. With locked phones, incompatible technologies between providers, and post-paid plans the norm for locals, tourists have been poorly catered for.

But what if you need to connect with your family in the US? Or if you want to call/text your travel companions while wandering the busy tourists spots in LA? Or maybe you’ve met some local friends who want to catch up with you later but need your US no. to text or call you? Or you just want to be on social media for a blow-by-blow detail of your trip?

Well, that’s where CellComCentral comes in! We have a solution for that!

CellComCentral was initially formed through recognizing this communications gap among family members and friends who came here to the states to visit and their first questions or requests would be: Where can I buy a prepaid sim card? Will my smartphone work on that card? If not, do you have any extra smartphone I can use?

CellComCentral’s vision is to become a highly distinguished and recognized leader in smartphone rentals and prepaid SIM card provider for visiting Filipino tourists. Our mission is to provide innovative and flexible communications experience.
